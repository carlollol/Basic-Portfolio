About Me

Carlo is a designer/illustrator based in Los Angeles, CA.


He spends his daytime doodling and creating awesome stuff at Telescope Inc. He has a passion for branding, illustration, user interface/web design and print. He is also an avid sports fan who likes to play basketball. On his leisure time, he either plays videogames or read business and startup books to continue to grow his knowledge.


He has previously helped build and grow several startups such as All-Star Fitness Kids LLC, Clearnroom Design LLC, and Strictly Gains Inc. and continues to maintain and expand these brands.
